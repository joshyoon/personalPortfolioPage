About Me

Hello, my name is Josh Yoon and I am a self-taught front end developer. I started to learn to code on the side as a hobby and I have fallen in love with learning code ever since. I always wanted to attend a coding bootcamp but money was tight while I was learning so I had to make due with the abundant free resources online. Although the self-learning route is not ideal for those who want a quick career change, never underestimate the power of learning through trial and error! If anyone is interested in learning code and brave enough to take the self-learning route like I did feel free to reach out using my contact information on this page.
